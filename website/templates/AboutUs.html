About Us


In accordance with the "The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013" of the Supreme Court of India of December 2013, we setup an online application, a Women's Cell at Panipat Institute of Engineering and Technology, committed to enable women of the institute to pursue their work with dignity and confidence. The aim of this web application, Women's Cell is to maintain a safe workplace for women and a harmonious atmosphere , and to raise awareness in the community on gender equality issues.
